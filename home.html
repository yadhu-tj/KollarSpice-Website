At Spice Kollar, we bring you pure, authentic spices from the misty hills of Idukki, where nature and tradition thrive. Grown in mineral-rich soil and harvested at peak potency, our spices are nurtured using age-old, sustainable practices. Each batch is minimally processed to preserve its bold aroma, natural oils, and healing essence. From seed to kitchen, every spice tells a story of care, heritage, and flavor—straight from the heart of the Western Ghats to your table.

CARDAMOM

Kollar Spices presents Rajakumari's emerald treasure - cardamom pods bursting with citrusy sweetness and floral depth. Grown in Idukki's shade-grown forests, each green capsule holds aromatic oils that elevate everything it touches. Kollar's careful sun-drying preserves the delicate balance of eucalyptus warmth and minty freshness.

CINNAMON

Rajakumari Cinnamon, nurtured in Idukki’s misty highlands, embodies purity and warmth. Hand-rolled by skilled farmers, its fragile quills release a honeyed aroma with hints of citrus and spice. Unlike common variants, this cinnamon is delicate, sweet, and subtly floral—perfect for elevating desserts, teas, and curries. Each harvest reflects generations of expertise, with sun-dried bark curling into paper-thin layers of golden-brown.

GINGER

Ginger from Rajakumari’s fertile Idukki soil bursts with fiery, earthy warmth. Sun-dried to perfection, its gnarled roots pack a punch—spicy, citrusy, with a lingering sweetness. Fresh or powdered, it enlivens teas, curries, and remedies. Locals treasure its golden hue and pungent aroma, a testament to organic farming.

CUMIN

Cumin from Rajakumari’s sun-baked fields carries an earthy, nutty depth with a whisper of citrus. These tiny brown seeds, roasted or ground, unleash warmth—smoky, peppery, and slightly bitter. Tempered in ghee, they perfume biryanis and dals, while whole seeds crackle in tadkas. Farmers dry them under Kerala’s fierce sun, locking in boldness.

PEPPER

Pepper from Rajakumari’s misty hills, curated by Kollar Spices, is a jewel of heat and aroma. Sun-dried on bamboo mats, each wrinkled berry holds fiery warmth with floral undertones—bold yet nuanced. Handpicked at peak ripeness, these black gold pellets elevate curries, marinades, and remedies.

Turmeric

Golden turmeric from Rajakumari's volcanic soil, nurtured by Kollar Spices, radiates with earthy warmth. Sun-dried rhizomes yield a vibrant powder - musky, slightly bitter, with orange-hued magic.
