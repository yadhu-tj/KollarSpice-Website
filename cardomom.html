Origin & Cultivation

Thriving in Rajakumari's unique microclimate (800m above sea level), our cardamom benefits from cool mountain air and rich volcanic soil. The high-altitude cultivation results in denser, more aromatic pods with higher essential oil content. Each plant grows under natural forest canopy, and pods are harvested at dawn when their aroma peaks, then slowly dried in traditional bamboo chambers to preserve their complex flavor profile.
